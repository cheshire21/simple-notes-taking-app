# Product Mission

## Problem

People struggle to keep their notes organized. Existing tools are either too complex, bloated with features, or don't offer a clean way to group notes by category in a single focused view.

## Target Users

Anyone who needs a simple, private space to capture and organize their thoughts — students, professionals, or anyone managing personal notes day-to-day.

## Solution

A clean, fast, category-based notes app. Users register and log in to their own private space. Notes are organized by categories visible in a sidebar. The interface is minimal and focused — one view, everything visible, no clutter. Creating and editing notes happens in a full-screen modal so nothing distracts from writing.
